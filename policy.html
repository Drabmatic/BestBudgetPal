Effective date: February 11, 2026 Package: com.bestbudgetpal

  • Budgeting data you enter in the app (transactions, budgets, bills, preferences).

  • Optional analytics and diagnostics events if enabled in the app build.

2. How We Use Information

  • To provide core app features (tracking expenses, budgets, reminders, and reports).

BestBudgetPal is local-first. Your budgeting records are stored on your device. If you export data, files are saved to the location you choose.
